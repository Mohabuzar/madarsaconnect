Privacy Policy for Madarsa Connect

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Madarsa Connect. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose your information when you use our application. This policy applies to all users of our app, including Heads (Admins), Faculty (Teachers), and Students.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that is essential for the functionality of our educational management platform. The type of information we collect depends on your user role.

  • Personal Information: We collect your full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and gender during registration and profile setup.
  • Academic Information: This includes your course details, attendance records, marks, quiz results, and any certificates issued through the app.
  • Financial Information: For fee management, we collect information related to your payment status, transaction details, and fee history. We do not store sensitive payment details like credit card numbers; they are handled by our secure payment gateway.
  • Technical Information: We collect your device's Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token to send you important push notifications related to app activities like new quizzes or fee reminders.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the services offered by Madarsa Connect. The primary purposes are:

  • To Provide Core Services: To manage user accounts, track attendance, manage fees, conduct quizzes, and share academic results.
  • To Facilitate Communication: To send important notifications, reminders, and updates about academic activities or administrative matters.
  • For Administrative Purposes: To allow the Head/Admin to manage faculty, students, courses, and overall institutional operations.
  • To Maintain and Improve our App: To analyze usage patterns, troubleshoot issues, and enhance the user experience.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data very seriously. All information collected by Madarsa Connect is stored on secure cloud infrastructure provided by **Google Firebase**.

  • Data Storage: Your data, including profiles, academic records, and other information, is stored in Firebase Firestore, a secure database.
  • Media Storage: Files such as profile pictures are stored in Firebase Storage.
  • Security Measures: We use industry-standard security measures, including data encryption and secure access protocols, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Access to data is restricted based on user roles (e.g., a student cannot see another student's marks).

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within the Institution: Your information is visible to other users within your institution based on their roles. For example, your attendance and marks are visible to your Faculty and the Head.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers (like our payment gateway) who assist us in operating our application. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your information for any other purpose.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access and update your profile information through the app's profile screen. If you wish to correct any inaccuracies or have your data deleted, you may contact your institution's Head/Admin or reach out to us directly through the contact information provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

Madarsa Connect is intended for use by educational institutions. If a student is under the age of 13, we require consent from a parent or legal guardian for the collection of their data, which is managed by the institution (Madarsa) upon enrollment.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
